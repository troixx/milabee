Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer refunds?

No. All services and retail product sales are final.

What happens if I am late to my appointment?

Appointments that are 10 minutes late or more will be canceled, and deposits are non-refundable.

Do you allow children in the salon?

Children under the age of 6 are not permitted.

How far in advance do I need to cancel my appointment?

At least 48 hours prior to your scheduled appointment.
