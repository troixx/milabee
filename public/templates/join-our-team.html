Welcome to Mila Bee Studios

Mila Bee Studios is a luxury beauty studio designed for licensed professionals who value professionalism, consistency, and long-term growth. Our studio provides a structured, high-standard environment where beauty professionals can focus on their craft while building sustainable careers.

Studio Culture & Expectations

Mila Bee Studios operates as a luxury studio, not a high-volume or hustle-based salon. We maintain clear expectations to protect the studio environment, our professionals, and our clients. Professionals are expected to arrive prepared, respect scheduling, maintain cleanliness, and uphold studio policies at all times.

Bonus & Incentive Plan

Bonuses are designed to reward performance, professionalism, and consistency. Bonuses are reviewed monthly and are not guaranteed.

  • Service Revenue Bonus: $100 to $300 based on monthly totals.
  • Client Retention Bonus: $50 for 80 percent or higher rebooking rate.
  • Retail and Add-On Bonus: 10 percent of monthly sales.
  • Referral Bonus: $150 per stylist after 60 days.
  • Consistency Bonus: $50 for policy compliance and punctuality (90 days).

Position Overview

Receptionist / Studio Coordinator

Responsibilities include booking, client communication, retail sales, front desk operations, and social media posting and engagement. Pay is $15 to $20 per hour with performance-based bonuses.

Professional Standards

  • Zero tolerance for unprofessional behavior, drug or alcohol use, unsanitary practices, or license non-compliance.
  • All professionals must display a valid license, maintain a clean station, and follow studio policies.
