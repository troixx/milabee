Salon Policies

To ensure a professional, clean, and respectful environment for both clients and stylists, the following policies are strictly enforced.

Client Policies

  • Appointments must be canceled or rescheduled at least 48 hours in advance.
  • A non-refundable deposit is required to secure all appointments.
  • If you are more than 10 minutes late, your appointment will be canceled, and your deposit will be forfeited.
  • No children under the age of 6 are permitted in the salon.
  • Clear communication is required regarding services, expectations, and scheduling.
  • All sales are final on retail products.
  • No refunds on services once rendered.

Stylist Policies

  • All stylists are expected to maintain professionalism, cleanliness, and punctuality.
  • Stations must be kept clean and organized at all times.
  • Respectful communication with clients and fellow stylists is required.
  • Timeliness for appointments is mandatory.
  • Maintaining a positive, collaborative salon environment is essential.
  • Failure to follow salon policies may result in termination of rental or commission agreements.
